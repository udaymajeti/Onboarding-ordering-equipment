We are Softhouse

We are a consultancy company that is excellent at developing solutions with software and developing people and businesses. We’ve been doing this since 1996. Today we are one of the leading companies in Scandinavia within Lean & Agile. We have a total of 200 employees at sites in Stockholm, Gothenburg, Malmö, Karlskrona and Växjö. Softhouse is a privately-owned Swedish company, that shown growth and profitability every year.

We help you grow

As a new employee, you get a mentor who helps you get started at Softhouse. You’ll also go to a bootcamp together with other new co-workers where you learn more about your professional role, our business approach and methodologies such as Lean & Agile, efficient software, etc. When on an assignment with a customer you are never alone: either you work in a Softhouse team or you work with an assignment coach. We help you progress through regular coordination of your personal development plan

We develop together

Each office holds regular seminars on new industry trends and you often have the chance to participate when our consultants hold lectures for external visitors

We build networks

The most important activities are “Lean Tribe”, a network of people who share values ​​that have emerged from Lean & Agile. We meet occasionally to share our experiences in simple forms—read more at leantribe.org!
